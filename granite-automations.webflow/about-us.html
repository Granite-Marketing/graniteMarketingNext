Why choose n8n?

n8n is our go-to tool for a reason. It’s open-source, developer-friendly, and endlessly customisable — perfect for building real production-ready automations. Whether you're going full no-code or need advanced logic with JavaScript or Python, n8n gives you the flexibility to do both without locking you in. With strong community support and native access to tools like LangChain, it's built for modern businesses ready to move fast.
